# Code of Conduct

FemmeVoice is a space for people of all gender identities, backgrounds, abilities, bodies, and voice goals.

Be kind, assume good faith, respect names and pronouns, and offer feedback about ideas rather than people. Harassment, hate speech, sexual content directed at others, doxxing, threats, or deliberately unsafe voice-training advice are not welcome.

Report concerns privately through the repository's security contact. Maintainers may remove content or participation that violates this code to protect the community.
